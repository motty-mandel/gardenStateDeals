About Us

Garden State Deals was founded on the values of hard work and entrepreneurial spirit. Growing up in a business-oriented family, I was inspired by my parents, who started their own company in the 1970s. Their dedication and resilience taught me the importance of integrity and customer commitment, principles that shape Garden State Deals today. With multiple warehouse locations and a focus on commercial clientele, we’ve established ourselves as a trusted buyer and seller of goods across various industries. We pride ourselves on maintaining a robust inventory, ensuring we’re always well-stocked to meet the needs of our valued, repeat customers. Whether sourcing new suppliers or handling large-scale orders, Garden State Deals is equipped to serve the diverse needs of our clients with flexibility and professionalism.

Inventory Management

At Garden State Deals, we pride ourselves on a strategic approach to inventory management that ensures we’re always ready to meet the demands of our commercial clients. With extensive warehouse facilities and a commitment to keeping high stock levels, we’re equipped to support continuous and large-volume orders across industries. Our advanced inventory systems enable us to track and maintain the flow of goods efficiently, ensuring that essential supplies are always available for our customers, whether they’re operating healthcare facilities, corporate offices, educational institutions, or IT companies. By prioritizing a reliable stock of high-demand items, Garden State Deals delivers consistency, reliability, and responsiveness that our partners depend on.

Industries Served

We support a diverse range of clients across various industries, ensuring that their unique needs are met with precision and care. Our expertise spans the following sectors:

  • Healthcare: Providing essential medical supplies and equipment to hospitals, clinics, and healthcare facilities to ensure they can deliver top-notch patient care.
  • Information Technology (IT): Supplying cutting-edge technology solutions and hardware to IT companies, helping them stay ahead in a rapidly evolving industry.
  • Corporate Facilities: Offering a wide range of office supplies and equipment to corporate clients, ensuring their operations run smoothly and efficiently.
  • Manufacturing: Supporting manufacturing plants with the materials and tools they need to maintain high productivity and quality standards.
  • Retail: Providing retail stores with a variety of products to meet consumer demands and enhance the shopping experience.
  • Hospitality: Supplying hotels, restaurants, and other hospitality businesses with the essentials to deliver exceptional service to their guests.

Our commitment to excellence and customer satisfaction drives us to continuously expand our offerings and improve our services to meet the evolving needs of our clients.
